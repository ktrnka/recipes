# Shortbread cookies

## Ingredients

<div class="ingredients" markdown="1">
- 1 1/4 cup butter, softened (2.5 sticks)
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 1/4 to 2 1/2 cup flour
</div>

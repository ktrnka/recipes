# Weight-volume conversions

- Flour: 1 cup = 120 grams (approx)
- Water: 1 cup = 227 grams (at what temp?)
- Egg: 1 large egg = 50 grams
- Egg yolk: 1 large egg yolk = 18 grams
- Oil: 1/4 cup = 50 grams, 1 cup = 200 grams
- Sugar: 1 cup = 200 grams
- Salt: 1 cup = 200 grams, 1 tbsp = 18 grams
- Yeast: 1 tbsp = 9 grams, 1 tsp = 3 grams
- Butter: 1 stick = 8 tbsp = 1/2 cup = 113 grams
- Heavy cream: 1 cup = 227 grams
